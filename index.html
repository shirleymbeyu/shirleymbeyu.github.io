Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and climate science. I have led and contributed to projects on agricultural sustainability, the causes and impacts of extreme climatic events, and strategies for adaptation. Across all my work, I emphasize usability and policy relevance, aiming to design solutions that are both implementable and impactful. Ultimately, my goal is to create research that translates into real-world benefits and contributes to the betterment of human life.
